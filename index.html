Questions

The things people ask before starting.

Is this just ChatGPT?+

No. We interview you for your core beliefs, formative stories, and the outcome you want each piece to create — then ground every article in that (and your book, if you have one). Generic AI sounds like everyone; the whole point here is that it sounds like you. And nothing goes out until you approve it.

Is it really my voice?+

We calibrate to your tone at onboarding, and if you share writing samples or a book, we ground the writing in them. You approve the topics up front and every article after — so the voice stays yours.

What if I don't like a piece?+

You review everything before it goes anywhere. Don't like one? Reject it and we rewrite. No piece is ever published without your yes.

Do I own the content?+

Completely. Everything we write is yours to publish, edit, or repurpose however you like.

How does the free trial work?+

Fill in the 5-minute questionnaire, we write three articles in your voice, and you decide from there. No card required.

Do you publish it to LinkedIn or my website?+

Not yet. Today we deliver each approved piece to your inbox for you (or your team) to post. Direct publishing to LinkedIn and websites is on our roadmap.
